Intro

My name is Carla Madriz and I am currently a 4th year student studying Computer Science. I am from the Bay Area, but decided to go to CSUF for my undergrad. I am a first-generation Latina.

As a first-generation Latina, my journey into the world of computer science has been both exciting and challenging. Computer science, to me, represents the endless possibilities and opportunities that technology brings to our lives.

Being the first in my family to pursue higher education, I understand the unique hurdles that come with this journey. I see computer science as my way of making a difference, not just in my own life but also in my community. It's a field where innovation and creativity thrive, and where I can contribute my unique perspective as a Latina to solve real-world problems.

My journey in computer science is a source of pride for me and my family. I hope to inspire others from similar backgrounds to embrace this field and show that it's possible to overcome challenges and succeed. I believe that diversity in computer science is not just a goal but a strength, and I'm excited to be a part of it.
